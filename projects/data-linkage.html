The challenge

Why data linkage is essential

Self-reported surveys are invaluable, but memory lapses and missing records can obscure the truth. Our linkage strategy brings objectivity and depth.

Objective validation Cross-check maternal health, medication, and service use against official registries like SIM and SINASC.
Care access tracking Monitor prenatal visits, hospitalisations, and specialised care to spotlight service gaps.
Crisis context Blend cohort data with environmental events to understand how floods or pandemics shape wellbeing.
Security protocol

Ethics that go beyond compliance

Families trust us with their stories. We honour that trust by exceeding national standards for data protection.

Restricted environments

Linkage happens within secure Restricted Work Environments (ATR), limiting exposure of identifiable data.

Immediate de-identification

Identifiers are stripped post-match, replaced with encrypted pseudonyms stored separately from research datasets.

Minimum access

External partners only receive the essential variables required to perform linkages‚Äînever the cohort's clinical data.
