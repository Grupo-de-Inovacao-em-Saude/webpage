Researcher Profile GPIS

Cauane Blumenberg Silva

Data scientist and epidemiologist connecting advanced analytics to population health decision-making.

Data science Digital epidemiology Health inequities Applied statistics

Cauane Blumenberg Silva integrates computer science and epidemiology to unlock insights from complex public health datasets. He completed a PhD in Epidemiology at the Federal University of Pelotas (UFPel) and holds a master's degree in Computer Science from UFRGS, positioning him at the intersection of health informatics and statistical innovation.

At the International Center for Equity in Health (ICEH), Cauane leads teams that design data pipelines, dashboards, and decision-support tools for large-scale studies. His work spans survey methodology, reproducible analytics, and the modeling of behavioral and social determinants of health.

Beyond academia, Cauane founded Causale Consultoria, where he partners with organizations to improve data collection and analysis strategies.
