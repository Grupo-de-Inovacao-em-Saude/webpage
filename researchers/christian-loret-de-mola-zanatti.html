Christian Loret de Mola Zanatti

Full Professor at the Federal University of Rio Grande (FURG) and senior researcher at the GPIS Health Innovation Research Group.

Life-course epidemiology Mental health Birth cohorts Global collaboration

Christian Loret de Mola Zanatti is a physician and epidemiologist whose career bridges clinical practice, advanced quantitative methods, and collaborative public health research. A full professor at the Federal University of Rio Grande (FURG), he earned his PhD in Epidemiology from the Federal University of Pelotas (UFPel) and completed both doctoral and postdoctoral work there. He also holds a master's degree in Clinical Epidemiology from Universidad Peruana Cayetano Heredia in Peru.

Christian investigates the social and biological determinants that shape mental health, with special attention to depression, stress, and how early-life experiences influence wellbeing across the life course. His leadership in long-running birth cohorts and population health studies has produced evidence that connects childhood adversity, biological pathways, and adult mental health outcomes.

Through networks such as the CHANGE Research Working Group at Universidad Científica del Sur and international epidemiology consortia, Christian coordinates multi-country collaborations that translate longitudinal research into policies promoting mental health equity.
