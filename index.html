News & Updates

New Study Launched on 2024 Flood Impact

In response to the devastating 2024 floods, we have launched a new online follow-up study to assess the long-term mental health and social impacts on our cohort families.

Expanding Our Global Research Network

Our commitment to world-class research is strengthened by our international collaborations with leading institutions in the UK, USA, Peru, and across Brazil.

Targeting Perinatal Mental Health with Predictive AI

We have applied for a major grant from the Wellcome Trust to develop a new predictive model for perinatal depression and anxiety, aiming to identify at-risk mothers earlier.
