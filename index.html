Our Mission and Culture

The GPIS is defined by its horizontal structure and deep mutual trust, ensuring every member operates as an equal and fostering true autonomy. Our mission is dual-focused: generating high-quality scientific output (prioritizing quality over quantity) and achieving profound social impact through real-world applications to public health. This standard demands constant innovation, as we continually explore new and better research methodologies.

We are actively pushing boundaries by focusing on AI-powered research and advanced data methodologies. Our work currently includes deploying wearable cameras and AI-powered facial recognition to objectively analyze mother-child interactions, developing cutting-edge predictive and counterfactual models, and implementing sophisticated Data Linkage protocols with national health systems. This strategic blend of technological innovation and high scientific standards ensures our work is both cutting-edge and deeply relevant.
