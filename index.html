Our Mission and Culture

The GPIS is a highly collaborative research group defined by its horizontal structure and strong mutual trust, where every member operates as an equal and autonomy is valued. Our mission is dual-focused: achieving high-quality scientific output and ensuring profound social impact through real-world applications to public health. This high standard demands constant innovation, as we strive to continually explore new and superior methods of conducting and applying our research.

In 2025, we're pushing boundaries with AI-driven analytics and virtual reality simulations for health education, making our work more accessible and impactful.
